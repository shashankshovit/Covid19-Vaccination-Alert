Vaccine Availability Notifier

This tool checks for the vaccine availability every 5 minutes and notifies you as soon as your selected vaccine becomes available at a center. You can track multiple pincodes with your vaccine filters applied.

+
Note:
  • This tool works best on laptop/desktop. Mobile OS kills the browser tab, hence the script.
  • Please play/pause the notification sound and adjust the system volume accordingly.
  • Ensure that your system doesn't sleep/hibernate.
