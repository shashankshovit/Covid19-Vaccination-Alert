Vaccine Availability Notifier

This tool checks for the vaccine availability at a given interval and notifies you as soon as your selected vaccine and/or dose becomes available at a center. This new update lets you chose the time interval starting from 10 seconds to 30 minutes. You can track multiple pincodes with your vaccine filters applied.

Instructions:

  1. Enter your pincode. Apply filters.
  2. Click on the '+' button to add more rows if you need to track multiple pincodes.
  3. Delete unwanted rows by clicking '-' button at the end of row.
  4. Play/pause the audio to get familiar with the alert sound. Adjust the system volume accordingly. This alert audio is played automatically when the tool finds a center of your choice.
  5. Change the time interval as per your convenience (The default is 5 minutes).
  6. Click on 'Start' button.
  7. Relax.

+
Note:
  • This tool works best on laptop/desktop. Mobile OS kills the browser tab, hence the script.
  • Please play/pause the notification sound below and adjust the system volume accordingly.
  • Ensure that your system doesn't sleep/hibernate.
